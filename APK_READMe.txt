Instructions on how to run the application:
- You will need to have a VR cardboard (the VR kit from DSTA is good)
- Run the app and place the android phone into the VR headset
- To move around, turn and face the direction you would like to move in, then hold the button located on the top right of the VR headset (on the DSTA VR kit there's this little button on the top right which would press your phone screen)
- To be able to interact with the furniture in the showroom, face the furniture and press on the VR headset button
- You will be able to see a description of the furniture, its price along with the different varients it offers. You can click on the variants and watch the furniture change to it
- To visit another showroom, click on the buttons nears the doors of your current showroom
